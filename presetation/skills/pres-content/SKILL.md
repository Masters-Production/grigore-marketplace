# Presentation Content Skill

## Overview

This skill writes the complete presentation script based on the structure document, using the Hristosenko selling methodology.

## Input Required

- `brainstorming.md` - Audience and product details
- `structure.md` - Presentation structure

---

## CRITICAL TIMING RULE

**Start product presentation at 1 hour mark (1:15 max)**
- If content overflows: 4 elements + sales + 5th element + second sales
- Personal story BEFORE sales block
- Overflow elements AFTER sales block

---

## Core Methodology (MUST FOLLOW)

### The Content-Sales Paradox
**"В единицу нашего взаимодействия с человеком мы можем решить только одну задачу"**
(In one unit of interaction, we can only solve one task)

- You can either TEACH or SELL - not both simultaneously
- Content must serve sales, not replace it
- Replace "sales" with "persuasion" mentally

### The Ladder of Conviction (Лестница Убеждений)
Build conviction step by step:
1. Connect symptoms to the problem
2. Elevate problem to priority #1
3. Show your method is best
4. Show your product implements it best
5. Present specific offer options

### Content That Sells (NOT Educates)
For each content block:
- Talk about **WHY** it's important, not HOW to do it
- Show **SIGNIFICANCE** and **IMPACT**
- Give **INSIGHT** that there's an easier way
- **Make conclusions FOR them** (they'll make wrong ones otherwise)

### The Magic Pill Framework
**"Большинство людей, даже успешных... наивны и инфантильны"** (Most people, even successful, are naive and infantile)

- People seek ONE THING that solves everything
- Key word: **"САМО"** (automatically/by itself)
- "Одна штука, все остальное улучшается само" (One thing, everything else improves automatically)

**Examples:**
- "Fix energy leaks = unlimited energy for everything"
- "Master webinars = everything in your info-business grows"
- "Learn to choose right = everything changes automatically"

### Build Content from Product Backwards
**CRITICAL:** Match content to course modules exactly
1. Start with your course modules
2. Make steps/elements/mistakes MATCH modules
3. Show path in content → they agree → present course → SAME PATH!
4. "О сюрприз, путь-то такой же" (Surprise, it's the same path they already agreed to)

## Three Content Structures

### Structure 1: STEPS (Shagi)
"5 steps to [result]"

**Warning:** This structure obligates you to give educational content

**For each step, talk ONLY about:**
- WHY this step is important (not HOW)
- Examples of what happens when done wrong
- Simple solutions exist (but don't teach details)

**Example:** "Step 1: Choose the right property"
- DON'T explain criteria for choosing
- DO show examples of horrible choices and consequences
- SAY: "Correctly chosen property forgives your later mistakes - it's 50% of success"

### Structure 2: ELEMENTS (Elementi)
Components of success (not sequential)

**Advantage:** Less obligating to give useful content

**Example for "Dream Life":**
- Element 1: Self-esteem
- Element 2: Energy/State
- Element 3: Finances
- Element 4: Relationships

For each: Sell SIGNIFICANCE and IMPACT, not how to improve.

### Structure 3: MISTAKES/MYTH DEBUNKING (Oshibki)
"Main mistakes when trying to [goal]"

**Best advantage:** Automatically excludes need for educational content
- Add memes = entertaining + insightful
- Perfect for cold traffic

---

## Inside Each Step/Element (7-Step Framework)

1. **Show/prove why it's very important**
2. **Give content that either:**
   - Closes objections
   - Gives insight that there's an easier way
3. **Make conclusions FOR them**
4. **"If you don't handle [this], you will:"** [list negative consequences]
5. **"But when you handle [this], automatically:"** [list positive outcomes]
6. **Reinforce with a case study**
7. **Announce the course module** that addresses this topic

**Script for module announcement:**
"У нас специально для этого есть модуль на курсе, на котором мы будем это делать, и вы уже с такими результатами оттуда уйдете. Пошли дальше."

## Script Writing Guidelines

### FIRST 15 MINUTES (CRITICAL)

**The Netflix Reality:** 62% of Netflix users watch at 1.5x-2x speed. If entertaining content gets fast-forwarded, a 30-minute intro = DEATH for cold traffic.

**First 10-15 minutes must create ILLUSION of content starting**

#### Block 1: Sell the Webinar Itself (Program Slide)

**Use 4U Formula for bullets:**
Result + Quantity + Timeframe + How

**Progression example:**
- BAD: "How to lose weight"
- BETTER: "How to lose weight for women 35+"
- BETTER: "How to lose weight for women 35+ by 10-12 kg in 4 weeks"
- BEST: "...without gym torture and food restrictions, just by redistributing products throughout the day"

Each addition creates illusion of SECRET that others don't know.

#### Block 2: Promise Concrete Deliverables (2-3 slides)

"Кому было бы интересно, чтобы я сегодня показал..."
- My sales team motivation system that makes them overperform monthly
- My chatbot funnel scheme that brought 500 million
- The reel that got billion views

#### Block 3: End-of-Webinar Bonus

**For live:** Wheel of fortune, book giveaways (NOT discounts on training!)
**For autowebinar:** Easy bonus everyone gets

**CRITICAL:** Bonus topic must NOT compete with main offer
- Selling weight loss? DON'T give recipe menu (they think they have everything)
- Give something tangential they want

**Script:**
"У кого из вас такое было, что [pain point]? Мы приготовили методичку. В конце вебинара по кнопке сможете забрать."

---

### Vaccination Against Sales Rejection Framework

**The Problem:** 1.5 hours of value → suddenly "buy my course" = massive drop-off, hate

**The Dating Analogy:** Walking with no romantic context, suddenly trying to kiss = WEIRD. Must establish frame from start.

#### Step 1: Troubles Slide
"Трудности, с которыми сталкивается большинство людей, пытающихся [goal]"

**Not pains, but TROUBLES when they tried:**
- "Tons of contradictory information online"
- "One expert says this, another says that"
- "No support from environment"

#### Step 2: What This Leads To
- Stopped believing in yourself
- 25th gold medal in unfulfilled promises to yourself
- Don't even want to start anymore

#### Step 3: It's Not Your Fault (Optional)
"Это не ваша вина"
- Whole industry is business of extracting money
- Trainers benefit from you NOT getting results
- You never had a chance

#### Step 4: Therefore Our Product
"Именно поэтому мы сделали программу, которая позволяет..."
- Remove [result] in [timeframe]
- Even for those who tried 100 times
- Without [objection 1], without [objection 2]

#### Step 5: Coming Attractions
"Для тех, кому важен прорыв, я позже расскажу о программе. Что входит, какие результаты. И какое офигенное спецпредложение. Когда услышите - будете танцевать от счастья."

### Opening Section

**Hook Formula:**
```
[Provocative statement or question that challenges their current belief]

"Что если я скажу вам, что всё что вы знали о [TOPIC] - неправильно?"
"What if I told you everything you knew about [TOPIC] was wrong?"
```

**Credibility Formula:**
- Brief (30-60 seconds max)
- Results-focused, not story-focused
- "За последние X лет я помог Y людям достичь Z"
- Include personal/human elements (kids, family) = instant connection
- DON'T create unbridgeable gap (don't tell photographers about 100 billion business)

### Problem Agitation Section

**Amplification Formula:**
```
1. Name the pain they feel
2. Show it's getting worse
3. Reveal hidden costs they don't see
4. Create urgency to solve NOW
```

**Key Phrases:**
- "Знакомо ли вам когда..." (Do you know the feeling when...)
- "А что будет если так продолжать..." (What happens if this continues...)
- "Большинство делают эти ошибки..." (Most people make these mistakes...)

**Pain Language - EMOTIONAL, Not Literary:**

**WRONG (literary/empty):**
- "Твои отношения не такие экологичные, как хотелось бы"

**RIGHT (drunk girlfriend language):**
- "Почему, блин, раз за разом - либо мудак, либо тюбик, либо альфонс, либо мамкин сынок? Почему я красивая, умная, а живу как дура?"

**For Energy/State:**
- WRONG: "Не хватает сил. Чувствуешь себя уставшим."
- RIGHT: "Проснулся и уже устал. Без трех чашек кофе не можешь разлепить глаза. Пообедал и кожа на глазах натянулась."

**Alternative: Test Format for Pains**
Transform pain list into "test":
"Давайте проведем тест, насколько неустойчиво ваше финансовое положение"

Same bullets become TEST ITEMS with scoring:
- Low = situation not acute
- Medium = obvious problems
- High = SOS, priority #1

### Content Blocks

**Each Block Must Have:**

1. **Significance Statement**
   - Why this matters
   - What changes when you get it right

2. **Contrast Example**
   - Wrong way vs right way
   - Before/after story

3. **Belief Shift**
   - Old belief: [What they currently think]
   - New belief: [What they should think]
   - Bridge: "На самом деле..." (Actually...)

4. **Product Bridge**
   - "Это один из элементов моей системы..."
   - Natural transition, not forced sales pitch

**NEVER Do:**
- Give step-by-step instructions
- Teach the "how" in detail
- Make them feel they can do it alone

### Transition Section

**The Bridge Formula:**
```
"Итак, вы узнали [3 things]..."
"So you've learned [3 things]..."

"Но чтобы получить [RESULT], нужно ещё [GAP]..."
"But to get [RESULT], you also need [GAP]..."

"Поэтому я хочу пригласить вас..."
"That's why I want to invite you..."
```

**Vaccination Technique (Прививка):**
Before presenting offer, acknowledge:
- "Сейчас будет продающая часть..."
- "Я знаю что вы не любите когда продают..."
- Then explain WHY you're selling (to help them get results)

### Offer Section

**Module Presentation:**
- Name modules with MARKETING names (results-focused)
- NOT methodological names (process-focused)
- Example: "Быстрый старт" vs "Введение"

**Value Stack Formula:**
```
Module 1: [NAME] - обычно стоит [X]
Module 2: [NAME] - обычно стоит [Y]
Bonus 1: [NAME] - стоит [Z]

ИТОГО: [TOTAL]

Но сегодня вы получаете всё это за [PRICE]
```

**Price Reveal:**
1. Anchor high (what it's worth)
2. Compare to alternatives
3. Reveal actual price
4. Show savings percentage

### CTA Section

**CTA Formula (Repeat 15+ times):**
```
"Нажмите кнопку ниже" + [Unique benefit each time]

CTA 1: "Нажмите кнопку и получите мгновенный доступ"
CTA 2: "Нажмите кнопку чтобы начать [transformation]"
CTA 3: "Нажмите кнопку пока действует цена"
...
```

**Urgency Elements:**
- Time deadline
- Price increase coming
- Bonus disappearing

**Scarcity Elements:**
- Limited spots
- Personal attention constraint
- Exclusive for event participants

## Case Study Presentation Format

### Rules
- Minimum 5 cases BEFORE presenting program
- Show cases for ALL audience segments
- 90% of cases in CONTENT part, not selling part
- Include both exceptional AND average results

### Ideal Case Study Slide Structure

**Slide 1 - Before:**
- Worn-out photo of person
- City, age, profession (MORE IMPORTANT than name!)
- Key problems/requests in brief

**Slide 2 - After:**
- Happy photo
- What they achieved

**Slide 3 - Summary:**
- "Before → After"
- "In 1 year: [total results]"
- Large, readable format (mobile-first!)

### Case Introduction Script
"Я вам сейчас на конкретном примере реального человека покажу, как это работает и какой результат дает"

**NOT:** "Let me show you a case study"

### Video Case Rules
- Maximum 1.5 minutes
- Edit/cut ruthlessly
- Add subtitles
- Remove compromising phrases ("everything was clear to me in general")

### The "Ninja" Pre-Framing Technique
"Вам сегодня теорию или практику? Супер, дам много + покажу как работает на реальных примерах."

Now case studies don't trigger resistance.

## Engagement Framework

**Why it matters:** People only buy when ACTIVELY listening. If they're vacuuming, cooking = you're radio. They'll never buy from "radio."

### Engagement Sequence
1. Tech check: "Видно, слышно презентацию? Напишите"
2. Scale question with emotion: "Поставьте столько плюсов, насколько сильно хотите [goal]"
3. Frame-setting: "С какими проблемами я вам могу помочь?"
4. Read and respond to answers by NAME
5. Continue emotionally charged questions (1 per minute!)

### The Participation Contract
"Я даю вам контент без купюр. Вы даете мне включение и работу в чате. Не будет работы - ничего не будет. Руки на клавиатуру."

### Emotional Questions > Logical Questions
- WRONG: "Согласны? Напишите 1"
- RIGHT: "Поставьте столько плюсов, насколько вы ХОТИТЕ..."
- RIGHT: "Поставьте столько минусов, насколько вас БЕСИТ..."

### If Nobody Engages (Nuclear Option)
1. Stop completely
2. Call it out: "Мы что тут в партизан будем играть?"
3. Ask who WON'T participate no matter what
4. Set who's boss

## Specific Techniques

### Question Frequency
- "Один вопрос в минуту должен звучать" (One question per minute)
- If you think you're overdoing it, you're approaching right level
- Questions must be EMOTIONAL: "Кого бесит? Кто устал? Кому надоело?"

### Slide Strategy
- "Слайды бесплатные" (Slides are free)
- Don't put 10 minutes of content on one slide
- Make 10 slides with one sentence each
- Constant visual change keeps attention
- 80%+ watch from mobile - make slides readable!

### Position/Frame
**Wrong:** Seller begging buyer, dancing, performing
**Right:** "Вы ко мне пришли, потому что у вас есть проблема, а у меня есть решение"

### "Why Your Topic" Slide
Summarizing slide closing main objections:
"Почему вебинары?"
- Doesn't matter how many followers
- Can automate = money every day
- Works in all niches

### "Why Now" Slide (Concrete!)
- WRONG: "The sooner you start, the faster..."
- RIGHT: Mercedes E-class 3 years ago: 3.5M. Now: 12.5M. Money devalued 4x.
