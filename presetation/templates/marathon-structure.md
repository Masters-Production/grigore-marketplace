# Marathon Structure Template (3-5 Days)

## Overview

A marathon is a multi-day event that builds momentum toward a high-ticket offer.

| Day | Focus | Duration | Goal |
|-----|-------|----------|------|
| Day 1 | Foundation + Hook | 60-90 min | Excitement + Belief |
| Day 2 | Deep Dive | 60-90 min | Education + Trust |
| Day 3 | Advanced | 60-90 min | Mastery + Desire |
| Day 4 | Case Studies | 60-90 min | Proof + Confidence |
| Day 5 | Offer | 90-120 min | Sale + Action |

## Day 1: Foundation + Hook

### Purpose
- Create excitement
- Establish credibility
- Plant seeds for offer
- Build community

### Structure
```
[OPENING - 15 min]
- Welcome + Energy
- Big Promise of marathon
- What they'll achieve by Day 5
- Community rules

[CORE TEACHING - 40 min]
- Foundation concept
- "Big Idea" introduction
- First belief shift
- Quick win exercise

[ENGAGEMENT - 15 min]
- Q&A
- Homework assignment
- Preview Day 2
- Build anticipation

[CLOSE - 10 min]
- Recap
- Call to action (share, invite friends)
- Energy boost for tomorrow
```

### Key Scripts
```
"За эти 5 дней вы полностью измените..."
"Over these 5 days you'll completely transform..."

"Завтра мы пойдём глубже и я покажу..."
"Tomorrow we'll go deeper and I'll show..."

"Сделайте это упражнение до завтра..."
"Do this exercise before tomorrow..."
```

## Day 2: Deep Dive

### Purpose
- Deliver massive value
- Build trust through teaching
- Create "aha" moments
- Increase commitment

### Structure
```
[OPENING - 10 min]
- Welcome back
- Homework review
- Success stories from Day 1
- Today's promise

[CORE TEACHING - 50 min]
- Deep concept explanation
- Step-by-step framework
- Examples and case studies
- Second belief shift

[IMPLEMENTATION - 20 min]
- Live exercise
- Q&A
- Troubleshooting

[CLOSE - 10 min]
- Homework for Day 3
- Tease advanced content
- Maintain momentum
```

## Day 3: Advanced Techniques

### Purpose
- Show depth of knowledge
- Create desire for more
- Address objections indirectly
- Build anticipation for offer

### Structure
```
[OPENING - 10 min]
- Energy check
- Celebrate progress
- Showcase transformations
- Today's advanced topic

[CORE TEACHING - 50 min]
- Advanced strategies
- Insider secrets
- Third belief shift
- "The missing piece" concept

[DEMONSTRATION - 20 min]
- Live example
- Behind-the-scenes
- Real results breakdown

[CLOSE - 10 min]
- Homework
- Hint at "full system"
- Build curiosity for Day 4
```

### Transition Scripts
```
"Это одна часть моей полной системы..."
"This is one part of my complete system..."

"Те кто применили всю систему получили..."
"Those who applied the full system got..."
```

## Day 4: Case Studies + Proof

### Purpose
- Provide social proof
- Handle objections through stories
- Create FOMO
- Prime for offer

### Structure
```
[OPENING - 10 min]
- Recap journey so far
- Transformation stories
- Build excitement for tomorrow

[CASE STUDIES - 50 min]
- Student success story 1 (similar to audience)
- Student success story 2 (overcame obstacles)
- Student success story 3 (exceptional results)
- Pattern recognition

[OBJECTION HANDLING - 20 min]
Through stories:
- "I don't have time" story
- "I've tried before" story
- "I'm not sure it works for me" story

[CLOSE - 10 min]
- Tomorrow's big reveal
- Create massive anticipation
- "Завтра всё изменится..."
```

## Day 5: The Offer

### Purpose
- Present the offer
- Stack value
- Create urgency
- Close sales

### Structure
```
[OPENING - 15 min]
- Energy at maximum
- Recap entire marathon
- Show total transformation
- "You're ready" message

[VALUE RECAP - 20 min]
- Everything they learned
- Results achieved
- What's still missing
- The gap

[OFFER PRESENTATION - 40 min]
- Introduce program
- Module breakdown (outcomes focused)
- Bonuses reveal
- Value stack
- Price with anchoring
- Guarantee

[URGENCY + SCARCITY - 20 min]
- Limited time pricing
- Limited spots
- Deadline countdown
- Fast-action bonuses

[Q&A + CLOSING - 30 min]
- Live objection handling
- Success stories
- Repeat CTA
- Final deadline reminder
```

### Offer Scripts
```
"Вы прошли невероятный путь за 5 дней..."
"You've come an incredible way in 5 days..."

"Но это только 20% того что возможно..."
"But this is only 20% of what's possible..."

"Представьте если бы у вас была полная система..."
"Imagine if you had the complete system..."
```

## Marathon-Specific Elements

### Daily Engagement
- Homework between days
- Community interaction
- Progress celebrations
- Leaderboards/recognition

### Momentum Building
- Each day references previous
- Building complexity
- Increasing investment
- Growing desire

### Email Sequence
- Pre-marathon: 3-5 emails
- During: Daily reminder + replay
- Post-Day 5: Follow-up sequence

## Key Metrics

- Day 1 attendance: 40-60% of registrants
- Day 5 attendance: 20-30% of registrants
- Conversion rate: 8-20% of Day 5 attendees
- Average order value: Higher than webinar
