# Email Sequences for Presentations

## Sequence Types

1. **Pre-Event Sequence** - Before webinar/marathon/workshop
2. **During Event Sequence** - For marathons (between days)
3. **Post-Event Sequence** - Follow-up and sales
4. **Cart Abandonment** - For those who clicked but didn't buy

---
